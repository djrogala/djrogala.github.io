Tier II Pensions Explained

The 2025 cap on a Tier II pension is $127,283.01. This cap grows at a rate of 1/2 the CPI inflation rate or 3%, whichever is lower, resulting in a decrease in purchasing power over time. This is why you will see the pension value fall from ages 67 to 75 to 85.
